# PEA-Defense-Framework
The world's first open-source AI and IoT cybersecurity framework for small and medium businesses,  co-developed by contributors worldwide.
## 🔍 What Is This?
PEA Defense is a free, open-source cybersecurity framework 
designed specifically for small and medium businesses (SMBs) 
navigating the security risks introduced by AI tools and 
IoT devices.

## ❓ Why Does This Exist?
Most cybersecurity frameworks (NIST, MITRE, OWASP) were 
built for large enterprises. Small businesses are left 
without practical, accessible guidance — despite being 
the most vulnerable and the least protected.

PEA Defense fills that gap.

## 🎯 Who Is It For?
- Small business owners with 1–250 employees
- IT managers supporting small businesses
- Cybersecurity professionals advising SMBs
- Researchers and educators in cybersecurity

## 🌍 Our Goal
To become the first globally adopted, multilingual, 
open-source cybersecurity framework for SMBs, 
co-developed by contributors from multiple countries.

## 📌 Current Status
🚧 Framework under active development, Version 1.0 coming soon
